# Eiffel Tower Research Document

## Location
- Name: Eiffel Tower
- Address: Av. Gustave Eiffel, 75007 Paris, France
- Coordinates: 48.8584, 2.2945
- Rating: 4.7

## Key Facts (from Wikipedia)
- Built from 1887 to 1889 by Gustave Eiffel's company
- Nicknamed "La dame de fer" (Iron Lady)
- Height: 330 metres (1,083 ft)
- Base: square, 125 metres on each side
- 5,889,000 visitors in 2022
- Designated monument historique in 1964
- UNESCO World Heritage Site ("Paris, Banks of the Seine") in 1991
- Tallest human-made structure for 41 years until Chrysler Building (1930)

## French Translation
La tour Eiffel mesure 330 metres de haut et a ete construite entre 1887 et 1889. Elle a accueilli 5 889 000 visiteurs en 2022. Elle a ete class monument historique en 1964 et est devenue un site du patrimoine mondial de UNESCO en 1991.

## Engineer: Gustave Eiffel
- Born: 15 December 1832 in Dijon, France
- Died: 27 December 1923 (aged 91) in Paris, France
- Alma mater: Ecole Centrale Paris
- Notable works: Garabit Viaduct, Eiffel Tower, Statue of Liberty
- Name formally changed from Bonickhausen dit Eiffel to Eiffel in 1880
